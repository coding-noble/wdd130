Patterns

This page has four of my go to patterns when I want to diplay my Rubik's Cubes in a fun and unique way. Simply click on the pattern you would like to use and the algorithm for it will show below. When you are trying to memorize the algorithm try hiding it when you do it.

(U2 D2 F2 B2 L2 R2)

(U D' R L' F B' U D')

(F L F U' R U F2 L2 U' L' B D' B' L2 U)

(U' L' U' F' R2 B' R F U B2 U B' L U' F U R F')
